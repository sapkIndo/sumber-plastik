## 2024-11-20 - FAQ Accordion Accessibility Enhancement
**Learning:** For custom accordion components, `aria-expanded` on the trigger button should be paired with `aria-controls="[id-of-panel]"`, and the panel itself needs an `id`, `role="region"`, and `aria-labelledby="[id-of-trigger-button]"` to ensure screen readers properly associate the trigger with the content it expands/collapses. Furthermore, ensuring interactive elements have strong `focus-visible` states is critical for keyboard navigation without muddying the mouse-click experience.
**Action:** Updated `src/components/sections/FAQ.tsx` with proper ARIA attributes mapping the question buttons to the answer `<dd>` panels, and added `focus-visible` styles (`focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-blue-600 focus-visible:ring-inset`) for keyboard visibility.
